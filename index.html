This computational research project interrogates the dark synergy between quantitative linguistic patterns and physical curation within the Third Reich. By contrasting Albert Speer’s mathematical text parameters with visual databases and spatial 3D architecture, this installation exposes how authoritarian geometry weaponized the sublime philosophy of absolute order to validate systemic human exclusion.

II. Quantitative Text-Mining: Deconstructing Speer’s "Facts"

In his 1938 programmatic tract, "Facts and Lies" (tatsachen.htm), Reich Architect Albert Speer defended monumental urban projects under the semantic cloak of neutral structural "Truth." Computational string-frequency mapping maps how the regime intentionally substituted the discourse of political terror with sterile vocabularies of cosmic order. [cite: 2322]

III. Visual Juxtaposition: Canonical vs. Degenerate Archives

The linguistic metrics calculated from Speer’s text directly dictated the physical state censorship of fine arts [cite: 2312, 2323]. The Reich executed a calculated, dual-track curatorial layout: canonizing hyper-classical realism while organizing public shaming operations to liquidate avant-garde modernism [cite: 2323].

🏛️ CANONICAL ART RECORD (Official Reich Fine Art)
Rhetorical Sync Aligned with Speer's vocabulary of "Purity, Order, and Eternal Form" [cite: 2312, 2323]
Visual Motif Neo-classical statuary, idealized agrarian labor, and mathematically static landscapes completely devoid of individual human emotion [cite: 2323].
🔥 DEGENERATE ART RECORD (Banned Modernism)
Rhetorical Sync Branded as the chaotic antithesis—labeled as "Decay, Chaos, and Lies" [cite: 2323]
Visual Motif Expressionism, psychological distortion, and profound avant-garde critiques exposing the traumas of state militarism [cite: 2323].
