III. Visual Juxtaposition: Canonical vs. Degenerate Archives

The linguistic metrics calculated from Speer’s text directly dictated the physical state censorship of fine arts[cite: 2347]. The Reich executed a calculated, dual-track curatorial layout: canonizing hyper-classical realism while organizing public shaming operations to liquidate avant-garde modernism[cite: 2358].

🏛️ CANONICAL ART RECORD (Official Reich Fine Art)
Rhetorical Sync Aligned with Speer's vocabulary of "Purity, Order, and Eternal Form" [cite: 2347]
Visual Motif Neo-classical statuary, idealized agrarian labor, and mathematically static landscapes completely devoid of individual human emotion[cite: 2323].
🔥 DEGENERATE ART RECORD (Banned Modernism)
Rhetorical Sync Branded as the chaotic antithesis—labeled as "Decay, Chaos, and Lies" [cite: 2395]
